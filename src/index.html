Wildflower & Wonderful Cabin Guidebook

CHECK IN
  • Check in time is any time after 4:00p
  • You will receive a physical key, so check in times are managed by the Black Bear Resort
  • Address is 45 Wildflower Way, Cabin #30, Davis WV 26260

Gaining access:

  • Proceed to the Lodge once you arrive at Black Bear Resort.
  • Between 9a-5p Monday through Friday, go to the check in desk at the Lodge to get your physical key.
  • If you arrive after 5p or on a day when the Lodge is closed, there will be a physical key waiting for you in the “late arrivals” area of the Lodge.
  • Enter the door on the left side labeled “Late Check-Ins.”
CHECK OUT
  • Check out time is 11:00a
  • Due to limited staffing, late check-outs cannot be accommodated. We sincerely appreciate your understanding
  • Take all personal items, turn off lights, and return key to Lodge
  • If Lodge is closed, use drop box outside Late Check-in room
  • Before you leave:
  • October–April: leave thermostat on HEAT at 60° tp prevent frozen pipes
  • Fireplace: leave flue OPEN, ensure fire is out, close glass doors
  • Due to limited housekeeping staff, Black Bear Resort requires the following:
  • Place used towels in washer with detergent and start. OK to leaving running upon checkout
  • (Optional) Move to dryer before leaving
  • Strip used bed sheets and pillowcases and place in bathroom
  • Load dishwasher, add detergent, and start
  • Due to wildlife in the area, Leave bagged trash at edge of driveway between 9a-12p
  • If needed, there is a secured dumpster on Grizzly Way
BEFORE YOU ARRIVE / HOUSE RULES
  • Absolutely no smoking
  • Due to wildlife, leave bagged trash at driveway edge 9a–12p or take to dumpster
  • If windows are open, turn off HVAC
  • Upon checkout, leave heat ON at 60° (Oct–Apr)
  • As this is a dark skies area, keep exterior lights off 10p–8a
  • No parties
  • Any excessive mess requiring additional cleaning will result in an extra cleaning fee charged to the guest
  • Any damaged or broken items will result in replacement fee charged to the guest.

RESET WIFI

  • If internet is not working, first check for a cabin-specific issue:
  • Router and modem are in bedroom next to bathroom
  • Check surge protector light
  • If off → turn on and wait 5 minutes
  • If on → power off, wait 10 seconds, power back on
  • Wait 5 minutes before reconnecting
  • Note: internet infrastructure in Canaan Valley is limited. We have purchased an upgraded internet package but infrastructure issues are out of our control
FIREPLACE
  • Open glass doors fully
  • Open mesh screen
  • Ensure flue is open (pull flue handle towards you)
  • Flue must remain open while fire is going and upon leaving
  • Glass doors MUST remain open while fire is active
  • Close mesh screen once lit
  • Use supplied wood or purchase locally
  • Do NOT use fallen branches from the property
  • When finished: fire out, flue open, glass doors closed
KITCHEN APPLIANCES

Coffee Maker

  • To use the Keurig single cup coffee maker, add desired amount of water to the water holder in the top part of the machine
  • Select power “on”
  • Place mug below brewing area
  • Open K-cup section and insert a cup
  • Close K-cup lid
  • When the light flashes, press the giant “K” silver button and coffee will begin brewing

    • Toaster

  • To use the toaster at Wildflower and Wonderful, first make sure toaster is plugged into an outlet
    • • Set bread in the top of the toaster
  • Turn dial on the side to desired setting for time
  • Push lever on the side down to start the toaster
  • Toast will either pop up when the timer is done, or you can push the orange-lit button next to the dial to end toaster early

    • Microwave

  • Pull the handle towards you to open the microwave door
  • Insert your food or beverage in one of the microwave-safe dishes provided - do NOT use metal, plastic, Styrofoam, or other non-microwave safe containers
  • Use a pre-set time, or enter desired amount of time for cooking
  • Select “start”
  • Microwave will beep when done

    • Stovetop

  • To use a stovetop burner, place a pot or pan on the burner you wish you use
  • Select the knob for the corresponding burner and turn to desired temperature
  • Turn the knob to “off” when you have finished cooking

    • Oven

  • To use the oven, select “Bake”
  • Use the arrows to select desired temperature
  • Hit “Bake” again. Wait a few minutes for oven to warm up to desired temperature, it will beep when ready
  • When you are done with the oven, hit “OFF”

    • Dishwasher

    • Load dishwasher with dirty dishes. Do not include pots and pans - they are not dishwasher safe
    • Get a dishwasher tab from under the kitchen sink
    • Open dishwasher door
    • Open small detergent holder, insert dishwasher tab
    • Close dishwasher tab small holder, it will "click”
    • On the top edge of the door, select “Normal Wash”
    • On top edge of the door, select “START.” The green light next to “START” will go from blinking to steady green when “START” is selected.
    • Close the door, it will click into place and beginning running. It is VERY quiet when running.
    TV & ENTERTAINMENT
    • To turn on the TV, select “Power on/off” via the red button on the remote
    • TV auto loads Samsung TV+, which has 100's of channels
    • Use Guide for channels
    • To use streaming apps, press the Home button and sign in with your own streaming service

    Super Nintendo

    • Located in loft

    SHOWER, LAUNDRY & THERMOSTAT

    Shower

    • HOT is to the RIGHT (reverse of typical). There is also a label in shower
    • Pull knob out for showerhead

    Washer & Dryer

    • Located in bathroom
    • Laundry detergent tabs are on top of dryer. There is a stool in the kitchen if needed
    • Use cold + large load. Do not overfill
    • Dry on normal. Be sure to clean lint filter (on dryer door) before each load

    Thermostat

    • Located across from primary bedroom
    • Modes: OFF / HEAT / COOL. Press Mode to switch
    • Adjust with +/-
    • Please turn HVAC mode to OFF if opening windows (summers are very pleasant!)
    • Winter checkout: HEAT at 60° to prevent frozen pipes
    • Summer checkout: COOL at 78°
